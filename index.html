About This Journey

Why I Started
Writing It Down

I've reached a point in life where I finally have the time to travel more freely. This blog is a way to keep a record of where I go, what I see, and what I experience along the way.

It's also a way for friends to follow along, and maybe find ideas for their own trips.

I'm not trying to create a travel guide or a business. This is simply a personal journal — notes from the road as I go.
